Welcome

Our mission is to work with local councils and organisations to design and build smart farms that grow fresh, nutritient-rich produce at minimal cost all year round for their communities.

By combining permaculture design with modern vertical farming technologies and a holistic approach to agriculture, we can improve food security, public health and enhance the beauty and biodiversity of our urban spaces. All while reducing dependence on destructive industrial practise.

We are committed to making a positive impact in the communities we serve. This is achieved by creating meaningful, social work, organising open harvest and training events, crowd-sourcing recycled materials and designing to minimise energy usage and waste production.

Everyone deserves access to friendly, healthy food and it shouldn't come at the cost of the planet. Let's make that happen!

What we do

  • Develop and maintain open-source smart-farming technologies.

  • Work with community organisations to design and build smart-farm systems.

  • Provide system monitoring, staff training and technical support.

  • Supply continuous learning via network events and our online information hub.
